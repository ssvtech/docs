## Viewing your plan

Go to **Settings → Billing** to see your current plan, usage, and next renewal date.

## Upgrading your plan

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**
2. Click **Upgrade**
3. Select a plan and billing cycle (monthly or annual)
4. Complete payment via Razorpay

Annual billing gives you approximately 25% off the monthly price.

## Topping up screening credits

If you run out of AI screening credits before your cycle resets:

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**
2. Click **Buy credits**
3. Select a top-up pack and complete payment

Credits are added to your account immediately after payment.

## Cancelling your subscription

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**
2. Click **Cancel plan**
3. Confirm cancellation

Your plan remains active until the end of the current billing period. After that your account reverts to Free.

## Invoices

All invoices are available in **Settings → Billing → Invoice history**.
