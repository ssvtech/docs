---
title: AI Screening
description: Score and summarize candidates automatically using your hiring profile.
---

AI screening compares each candidate's resume against your hiring profile and returns a score from 0 to 100 along with a one-line summary of fit.

## How to screen candidates

1. Open a job or go to the **Candidates** page
2. Select one or more candidates using the checkboxes
3. Click **Screen with AI**
4. Scores and summaries appear within a few seconds

## Understanding the score

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 80–100 | Strong match — meets most criteria |
| 60–79  | Moderate match — worth reviewing |
| 40–59  | Partial match — some gaps |
| Below 40 | Weak match |

## Screening credits

Each AI screening consumes one credit from your monthly quota.

| Plan | Credits / month |
|------|----------------|
| Free | 25 |
| Starter | 150 |
| Growth | Unlimited |
| Scale | Unlimited |

<Note>
  You can purchase top-up credits from **Settings → Billing** if you run out before your cycle resets.
</Note>

## Re-screening

You can re-screen a candidate at any time if you update the hiring profile. Re-screening consumes an additional credit.
