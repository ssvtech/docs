Talenzly is an AI-powered recruiter copilot that helps you screen, score, and manage candidates — faster than traditional ATS tools.

## What you can do

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Upload candidates" icon="upload" href="/features/upload-candidates">
    Bulk upload resumes via PDF or Excel. Talenzly parses and stores them automatically.
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI screening" icon="robot" href="/features/ai-screening">
    Let AI score each candidate against your hiring profile in seconds.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Invite your team" icon="users" href="/features/invite-team">
    Add recruiters and hiring managers with role-based access.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Share shortlists" icon="share" href="/features/shortlists">
    Send a read-only shortlist link to stakeholders — no login required.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Who is it for?

- **Solo recruiters** managing high-volume hiring
- **Recruiting teams** that need shared pipelines and role-based access
- **Agencies** handling multiple clients and job roles simultaneously
