---
title: Invite Your Team
description: Add team members with role-based access to your workspace.
---

<Note>
  Team members are available on Growth plan and above. Starter and Free plans are single-seat.
</Note>

## Roles

| Role | What they can do |
|------|-----------------|
| **Admin** | Full access — manage team, billing, jobs, candidates |
| **Member** | View and manage candidates and jobs; cannot access billing or team settings |

## How to invite someone

1. Go to **Settings → Team**
2. Click **Invite member**
3. Enter their email address and select a role
4. Click **Send invite**

They receive an email with a sign-up link. Once they register, they are added to your workspace automatically.

## Removing a member

1. Go to **Settings → Team**
2. Click the **⋯** menu next to the member
3. Click **Remove**

Removing a member immediately revokes their access.
