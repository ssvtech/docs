---
title: Shortlist Sharing
description: Share a read-only candidate shortlist with hiring managers or clients.
---

Shortlists let you share a curated list of candidates with anyone — no Talenzly account required.

## Creating a shortlist

1. Go to the **Candidates** page
2. Select the candidates you want to share
3. Click **Share shortlist**
4. Give the shortlist a label (e.g. "Final 5 — Backend Engineer")
5. Click **Create link**

## Sharing the link

Copy the generated link and send it to your hiring manager or client. The link opens a read-only page showing:

- Candidate name and email
- AI score
- AI summary
- Top skills
- Current status

## Link behaviour

- Links do not expire by default
- Recipients cannot edit, download, or contact candidates
- The page shows your company branding if configured (Growth/Scale plans)

<Note>
  Shortlist sharing is available on Starter plan and above.
</Note>
