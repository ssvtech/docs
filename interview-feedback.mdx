---
title: Interview Feedback
description: Collect structured feedback from interviewers via a shareable link.
---

Talenzly generates a unique feedback link for each interview round. Interviewers open the link, fill in ratings and notes, and submit — no login needed.

## Setting up an interview round

1. Open a candidate profile
2. Go to the **Interviews** tab
3. Click **Add round**
4. Enter the round name and interviewer name
5. Click **Save**

A unique feedback link is generated automatically.

## Sending the feedback link

1. Click **Copy link** next to the round
2. Send it to the interviewer via email or Slack

## What interviewers see

The feedback form collects:

- **Competency ratings** — scored 1–5 or marked N/A
- **Compensation & availability** — expected CTC, notice period, current CTC
- **Notes** — free-text observations
- **Recommendation** — Advance / Consider / Reject

## Viewing submitted feedback

Submitted feedback appears on the candidate's **Interviews** tab. An AI-generated summary of all rounds is shown at the top once at least one round has been submitted.
