title: Quickstart
description: Go from signup to your first screened candidate in under 10 minutes.
---

## Step 1 — Create your account

Go to [app.talenzly.com/register](https://app.talenzly.com/register) and sign up with your email.

## Step 2 — Create a hiring profile

A hiring profile defines the role you are hiring for and the criteria AI uses to score candidates.

1. Go to **Jobs** in the sidebar
2. Click **New job**
3. Fill in the job title, description, and required skills
4. Click **Save**

## Step 3 — Upload candidates

1. Open the job you just created
2. Click **Upload resumes**
3. Drag and drop PDF resumes or upload an Excel/CSV file
4. Talenzly parses each resume automatically

## Step 4 — Run AI screening

1. Select candidates from the list
2. Click **Screen with AI**
3. Each candidate gets an AI score (0–100) and a one-line summary
4. Sort by score to see your best matches at the top

## Step 5 — Move candidates through the pipeline

Use the status column to track each candidate:

`Applied → Screening → Shortlisted → Interview → Offer → Hired`

That's it — your pipeline is live.
